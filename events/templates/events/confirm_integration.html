How to Create a Razorpay Payment Page

Follow these steps to create a Razorpay Payment Page for your event:

  1. Log in to your Razorpay account. If you donâ€™t have one, create an account at razorpay.com and complete your KYC.
  2. Navigate to the 'Payment Pages' section on your Razorpay dashboard.
  3. Create a new Payment Page and customize it for your event.
  4. Important Properties to set while creating the payment page:
    • Page Title: Name your Page title and give the page a description of the event and tickets.
    • Create Tickets: If there are a limited number of tickets,click the price field, then Click the Item with Quantity option and give the name and price of the ticket. After that, click on the three dots to the right of the price and go to Advanced Options then click on Limited options in Units Available in stock,add the no. of tickets then below it, define the min and max number of tickets that can be purchased by a user in Quantity Limit per order. Preferably keep min as 0 and max as 1 if you want others to buy just one ticket. If you have more than one ticket, click on the price field below and repeate the same step with differernt name in the Price field.
    • Customer Fields: Ensure that the following fields are included for your payment form:
      • First Name
      • Last Name
      • Email
      • Phone Number (optional)
    • And to create these fields, click on the Input Field
    • Contact Us: Add your email and phone number in the Contact Us section
    • Redirect URL: Go to page settings on the top bar and check the Redirect to your website, after that a field will pop up where you will put the link/url of our site so that the user will automatically come to our website. Note: Remember not to give the link/url of this page but the link/url of the page when you first get into the site
    • Custom Message(Optional): If you want to show a custom message after successful payment, check the Show custom message and then type the custom message into the field
    • Page Expiry(Optional): If you want you could uncheck the ,No Expiry and a expiry date , after that date the usesr will not be able to access that page
  5. Payment Page Url: In the settings page, the top field URL of this page after the https://pages.razorpay.com/, you are allowed to write something, type in a comples combination of letters,numbers and special characters and save the page
  6. Save the Payment Page URL and copy it into the field in teh next page to integrate with your event.
